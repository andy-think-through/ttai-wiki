# Hike SES

> Search Everywhere Strategy documents for Hike SEO clients. Converts Manus visibility audit data into polished, client-ready strategy docs.
> Last updated: 2026-04-08

## Content

Think Through AI produces **Search Everywhere Strategy (SES) documents** for Hike SEO clients. These are polished strategic guides that synthesise Manus platform visibility audit data into actionable visibility recommendations across search channels.

### What It Is

Andy ingests raw Manus audit output (markdown format visibility data across organic search, paid search, local, review, and other channels) and transforms it into a professional strategy document—typically a polished PDF or branded deck—that Hike can present to clients.

The SES document covers:
- Current visibility landscape (organic rankings, paid position, review footprint, local presence)
- Visibility gaps vs competitors
- Prioritised action roadmap
- Channel-specific tactics
- Success metrics

### Commercial Model

This is a retainer/productised offering. Andy produces SES documents on a recurring basis for Hike's client base. There is no direct charge to Think Through AI; Hike retains control of pricing and client relationship.

### Andy's Role & Relationship

- **Retained directorship:** Andy retains a directorship at Hike (he co-founded the platform ~8 years ago with ~2,000 customers)
- **Contractual visibility:** Andy is contractually required to maintain visible association with the Hike brand externally
- **Constraint:** Cannot promote SEO services directly; focus is on AI and automation
- **Leadership:** Bryn is the new Managing Director of Hike; Andy stepped back from CEO to focus on Think Through AI

### Skill

The skill `search-everywhere-strategy` automates SES document generation. Triggered when raw Manus audit data is provided.
